Without softmax tying queries to keys, you re-associate the products: `(phi(Q) phi(K)^T) V -> phi(Q) (phi(K)^T V)`, turning O(L^2 d) into O(L d^2). The bracketed `phi(K)^T V` is a fixed **d x d state matrix** `S = sum k v^T` that does not depend on the query. So linear attention **is a linear RNN with a matrix-valued state**: training uses a parallel or chunkwise form (linear, matmul-friendly), and decoding maintains S recurrently (`S += k v^T; o = q S`) at O(1) time and memory per token, with a constant-size cache.
