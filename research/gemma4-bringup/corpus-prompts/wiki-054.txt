--- title: Valkey type: entity tags: [valkey, in-memory-db, replication, criu, upstream, kernel] sources: [valkey-dollysave-pr, valkey-udp-rdb-sync, bench-no-oom-rules, codex-mem-valkey] updated: 2026-06-30 --- # Valkey Valkey is the in-memory database (the Redis-fork open-source project) where the owner's upstream save/replication work lives. The headline effort is **DollySave**, a CRIU-based live-migration replacement for Valkey's `BGSAVE`: instead of fork-and-serialize, the process live-migrates — memory streams out while it keeps serving, with only a brief final freeze. The upstream-facing PRs are authored by [[asaf]] (asafpamzn, the owner's manager and a maintainer-facing author, for better landing odds), while [[avi]] did the research, the POC, and most of the implementation. See [[criu-live-migration]] for the mechanism and [[dirty-ring-wp-async]] for the freeze-time win that is the real intellectual property. ## DollySave (valkey PR #3608) valkey PR #3608 — "A new SAVE path for Valkey: DollySave - up to 35x faster, no COW, OOM-safe".
