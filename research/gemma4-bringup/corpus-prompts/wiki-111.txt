Decomposes "compaction policy" into **six largely orthogonal knobs** — data layout, granularity, picker policy, parallelism, I/O path, placement — plus a seventh hidden substrate (VersionSet/MANIFEST serialization) that no policy change touches, and elevates deletion to a fifth cost axis with its own RUM triangle. The through-line: production tiered engines have all quietly converged on Lazy Leveling (tier the small levels, level the largest), and the bottleneck has migrated from bytes-rewritten to syscalls-per-byte and placement. ## Key takeaways - **Closed-form amplification math:** with size ratio T and L ≈ log_T(N/B) levels — leveling WA O(T·L) / SA O(1/T); tiering WA O(L) / SA O(T).
