Each tool ships as its own npm package (`@agent-sh/harness-read`, `-write`, `-grep`, …) and an umbrella `@agent-sh/harness-tools` re-exports the whole surface. Matching Rust ports live under `crates/` (one crate per tool) and maintain parity with the TS implementations. The repo is published under the `agentsys` marketplace (described as "AI agent harness tools for Bedrock Converse and OpenAI-compatible APIs") and requires Node ≥ 20.
