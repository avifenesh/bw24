Three simulators dominate, each with a distinct philosophy. They are the engine you actually run the rigid-body equation `M(q)q̈ + C(q,q̇)q̇ + g(q) = τ` inside. A practical reality is that real labs run *two* of them — e.g. MuJoCo (CPU dev) → MJX (training), or Isaac Lab (training) → Drake (verification) — and keep an authoritative robot model in URDF+xacro with a regeneration script for the extra fidelity layer (MJCF or USD). The `mujoco_menagerie` (Apache 2 collection of canonical MJCF models — Franka, UR, KUKA, Spot, ANYmal, G1) is the de-facto reference repo everyone forks from. **MuJoCo** (Emo Todorov;
