--- title: banthis type: entity status: active tags: [agent-tooling, claude-code, skill, agent-memory, agent-sh] sources: [] updated: 2026-06-29 --- **banthis** is a small CLI tool and Claude Code skill published by the [[agent-sh]] org (`agentsys` marketplace) whose job is to persist "banned behaviors" into a project's `CLAUDE.md` or `AGENTS.md` so AI coding agents remember never to repeat an annoying pattern across sessions. The pitch is to turn a user's in-the-moment "stop doing this" into a permanent, high-priority negative rule. It is filed under agent-sh's authoring/meta tooling alongside [[skill-curator]] and [[system-prompt-curator]], shipping under the `@agent-sh/*` scope at version 0.3.1.[^1] It was registered into the agentsys marketplace around the v5.13/v5.14 window (the same wave that added `axiom`, the two curators, `ada-spark`, and `mojo`, briefly pushing the installable plugin count to 26 before v6.0.0 trimmed it back to 24).[^1] Mechanically, banthis maintains a managed section delimited by `<!-- banthis:start -->` / `<!-- banthis:end -->` markers inside the target memory file.
