--- title: latent-bridge type: entity status: active tags: [diffusion-llm, knowledge-head, latent-loop, bridge-controller, research, paper] sources: [latent-bridge-project] updated: 2026-06-29 --- # latent-bridge latent-bridge (`~/projects/latent-bridge`) is a research project, aimed at a small model plus a paper, that composes a DiffusionGemma knowledge head with a Qwen3.5-4B reasoner through a trained bridge-controller, forming a closed LATENT loop. The bridge meets the prompt and owns control flow via four learned functions: rho (router), f (forward), g (backward), h (halt). The latent loop is existential — a text loop would just be a pipeline and worthless — and frozen purity was dropped as a constraint. The value bar: the composed system must beat a same-class monolith (Qwen3.5-4B) on knowledge-heavy tasks. The owner drives design; the LLM helps, verifies, and implements (see [[working-style-lead-helper]]). ## Status Active. Phase 0 spike is DONE (2026-06-13, all 4 steps PASS) and the knowledge-head premise holds.
