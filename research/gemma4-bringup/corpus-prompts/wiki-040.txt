--- title: EXLA CPU-only build requires EXLA_CPU_ONLY=1 type: source tags: [exla, elixir, xla, cuda, build, nx-playground, gotcha] updated: 2026-06-29 raw: raw/memory/exla-cpu-only-build.md --- # EXLA CPU-only build requires EXLA_CPU_ONLY=1 EXLA (the Elixir XLA backend) on a box with `nvcc` on PATH auto-compiles CUDA code paths in its Makefile, and the NIF then fails to load with `undefined symbol: cudaGetErrorString` — unless `EXLA_CPU_ONLY=1` is set at build time. `XLA_TARGET=cpu` only controls the xla_extension binary download, not the NIF compile; both flags are needed for CPU.
