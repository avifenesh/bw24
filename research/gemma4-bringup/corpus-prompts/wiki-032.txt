NVIDIA validated the recipe by pretraining a 12-billion-parameter hybrid Mamba-Transformer (the Nemotron-Nano-12B-v2-Base architecture — 62 blocks of 6 self-attention, 28 FFN, 28 Mamba-2, hidden 5120, FFN 20480) on **10 trillion tokens**, the longest publicly documented 4-bit training run to date. The result lands essentially on top of an FP8 baseline: 62.58% vs 62.62% on MMLU-Pro 5-shot, with most downstream benchmarks comparable and the largest gap appearing on coding (HumanEval+ 57.43 vs 59.93, MBPP+ 55.91 vs 59.11), which the authors partly attribute to noisy final-checkpoint evaluation.[^1][^2] The payoff is hardware
