The through-line: a perf claim is worthless without enough independent runs to separate signal from variance, and a "win" that regresses the normal case is not a win. ## Baseline and the normal case Establish a long-term baseline **first**, before any change. The baseline must defend the **normal case** (values <1KB, set/get 20/80, high QPS) — a finding only counts with no regression there. Large/array payloads are secondary; never let a big-payload win mask a normal-case regression. No gain -> no change, unless the win is provable in the compiled binary (fewer instructions / eliminated malloc visible in asm or alloc trace). ## N>=5, medians, and variance Run each condition with **N>=5 separate process launches**, compare **medians**, report min..max.
