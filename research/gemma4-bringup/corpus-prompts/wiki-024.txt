The verdict is neither the position paper's fragile optimism nor doom: monitorability is a **contingent, defendable, instrument-tracked property**, and the reason it holds — *necessity* (hard serial computation must externalize, so hiding it costs performance) — is the same structural bet that keeps every channel defensible, from token CoT to activation space to steganographic paraphrasing. ## Key findings - **"Faithfulness" is three measurements, not one scalar.** Verbalization tests (Turpin 2023, Chen 2025) intervene on the *input*; counterfactual tests (Lanham 2023) on the *CoT text*; parametric unlearning (FUR, Tutek EMNLP'25) on the *weights*.
