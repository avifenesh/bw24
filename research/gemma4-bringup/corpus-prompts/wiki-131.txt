--- title: Claude Desktop + Cowork on Linux type: source tags: [claude-desktop, cowork, linux, electron, microvm, cloud-hypervisor, appimage] updated: 2026-06-29 raw: raw/memory/claude-desktop-linux-cowork.md --- # Claude Desktop + Cowork on Linux Documents porting the macOS Claude desktop app (v1.11847.5, Electron 41.6.1) and its Cowork VM agent-sandbox feature to Linux. The app itself was repacked from the macOS dmg into stock Electron and runs. The Cowork microVM compute backend was independently reverse-engineered and proven working on cloud-hypervisor+KVM, then the effort was superseded by adopting the maintained `stslex/claude-desktop-linux` repo, which solves Cowork on Linux via direct host execution (no VM). The standing lesson: the assistant independently rebuilt ~90% of stslex (VM proof + protocol RE + direct-exec service) before the owner pointed at the GitHub repo — next time, search GH for an existing port FIRST. ## Key takeaways - App port: extracted app.asar from the dmg HFS, repacked into stock Electron's `resources/` layout (so `isPackaged=true`), rebuilt node-pty for Electron 41 ABI, renamed the electron binary to `claude-desktop`. Installed at `~/.local/share/claude-desktop`, launcher `~/.local/bin/claude-desktop`. claude-native/claude-swift natives are darwin-only and fail-soft. Needs `--no-sandbox` unless chrome-sandbox
