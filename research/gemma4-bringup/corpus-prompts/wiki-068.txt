--- title: Epoch AI type: entity status: active tags: [organization, benchmarking, evaluation, ai-research, nonprofit] sources: [] updated: 2026-06-30 --- Epoch AI is an independent research organization whose stated mission is to inform the public about the trajectory of artificial intelligence through accurate, easy-to-cite data and analysis.[^1] It is best known for three product lines: a **data hub** tracking notable AI models, large-scale models (over 1e23 FLOP of training compute), and ML hardware accelerators; a long-form research arm; and an **AI Benchmarking Hub** of independent capability evaluations.[^1] Over 2024 the data hub added 170+ training-compute estimates, 75+ hardware accelerators, and 130+ benchmark evaluations, and its figures have been cited by Satya Nadella, Sundar Pichai, the US Bureau of Industry and Security, and the US Congressional Budget Office.[^1] The organization is donation-funded and announced a $10M fundraise over 2025–2026, planning to grow to roughly 27 full-time staff.[^1] Epoch's highest-profile output is **FrontierMath**, a private benchmark of over a hundred hard math problems built with 70+ mathematicians (including consultations with three Fields Medalists and an IMO expert).[^1] The benchmark was sponsored by OpenAI,
